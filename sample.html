Fire Plan / Assessment / Fire Points
CategoryAmber
ObservationThere is a Fire Plan in place with all Fire points seen to be well positioned and in date. It was noted that the smoke detectors for the accommodation block have been covered to mitigate against activation from dust. This was requested by the Police Estates Officer. It has been agreed that all are covered during the day and 2 uncovered in the corridor at the end of the day so that they are functional after hours. This process is in place, however, I advise that the isolation and de-isolation of the these two sensors is controlled under a permit to work, as a safety system is being inhibited. These should be issued and closed out each day to record that the safe system of work is in use.
Corrective ActionDiscussed with Nick and agreed that these isolations be controlled under a permit.Corrective Action Date20/07/2023

Dust/Noise
CategoryAmber
ObservationThe control of dust was seen to be very good across both work sites. There was no dusty works taking place, however it is clear that there will have been. There were dust cubes and M class vacuums available for use when needed. As an observation, it was noted that there is a Henry vacuum in one of the rooms which has been used for housekeeping, but these are not suitably rated for picking up dust and may put the user at risk of exposure. I appreciate that it is only used for housekeeping with low levels of dust as an alternative to sweeping, but an M or H class vacuum should be used.
Corrective ActionPlease ensure an M class vacuums is used for housekeeping in areas with construction dust to minimise the risk of exposure.Corrective Action Date20/07/2023
